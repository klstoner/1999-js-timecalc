Time Sheet Calculator Instructions

  • Type in when you started and ended work each day, as well as the amount of lunch taken total time in hours and minutes). Press Tab to move from field to field.
  • If you didn't work one day, don't enter any values in the fields for that day.
  • And don't forget to make sure that "AM or "PM" is checked, as appropriate. But don't check "AM or "PM", for days that you didn't work
  • Click "Calculate Hours" to see the total hours for each day and the total week.
  • Enter your "normal hours" in the appropriate field and click "Calculate Overtime" to see how much OT you accrued.
  • Click "Print Results" for a printable sheet of your hours
